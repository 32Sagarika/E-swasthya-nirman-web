Single Identity Digital Smart Card for Residents

Benefits to Citizens

  • Ability to access their medical records anytime, anywhere at global level
  • Instant availability of medical records during emergency situations
  • Ensures quicker diagnosis leading to accurate treatment and reduced risk of cross-medication
  • Immediate access and tracking of vaccinations for infectious disease
  • In case of Emergency, the Citizen can click the ‘HELP’ button and immediately send GPRS location to 5 pre-added Emergency Contacts in the Mobile App.
  • Get up-to-date knowledge of new government health schemes and benefits
  • Receive Health tips and information for preventive health care.
  • Citizen can join Blood/ Organ Donation Programs.
