About Us

eHealthSystem Technologies

Together, we’re on a path to wellness for body, mind, and spirit. Now anyone can take charge of their health and transform their life using our easy-to-use, yet powerful, healthcare technologies & tools.

The eHealthSystem supports WHO eHealth Strategy (WHA 58:28) eHealth stressing that eHealth is the cost-effective and secure use of information and communications technologies in support of health and health-related services.

eHealthSystem works as a LiveWire of Connectivity connecting different healthcare stakeholders that include clinics, pharmacies, blood banks, hospitals, ambulances, organ donors, laboratories and patients on a single platform.

We are a digital healthcare solutions provider. We dedicate ourselves to improving the existing Electronic Health Record technology further, by providing users the power to carry their health records globally in a portable, user-friendly and secure manner.

eHealthSystem is driven by visionary leaders and a passionate team of co-dreamers whose only aim is “To foster wellness for Body, Mind and Spirit” by way of offering innovative technology in healthcare delivery, beyond medicine, which is uniquely tailored, pragmatic, holistic and enduring. In short, changing the way how healthcare is managed across the world.

In order to achieve this, we’ve developed a Unique Cloud-based ‘eHealthSystem’ with an objective to create a Secure Healthcare Ecosystem that enables Global Interoperability for Unified Population Health Management and Delivery. Our “eHealthSystem” is Internationally Patented and complies with international standards such as HIPAA, HL7 and HITECH Acts.

eHealthSystem has a dedicated team of internationally acclaimed scientists, researchers, clinicians and doctors through its global collaborations. eHealthSystem’s rainbow of services includes Stem Cell Research, Innovative Research Center, Regenerative Medicine for Diabetes, Bone Marrow Registry and Repository, Cancer Research Center, Lifestyle Solution Center, Organ Transplant Center, Cord Blood Banking, Global Patient Registry, and Hospitals.
